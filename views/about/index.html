Two plus two equals five. "Who controls the past controls the future. Who controls the present controls the past." -George Orwell, 1984

Though this quote famously written by Orwell was used in description of a dystopian society, it still holds to be true in a different sense of the world today. I am Kamilah Nur, and I am a resident of both the Brownsville and Bedstuy neightborhoods of Brooklyn New York. As George Orwell says, someone who is in a position of control has the ability to change the world around them and make others have a new outlook on life. That is a position I always want to be in.
